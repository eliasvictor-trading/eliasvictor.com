About

Elias Victor, LLC is a California-based trading technology company focused on building automated indicators and strategies for futures markets. Every tool is developed with a single principle: systematic, rules-based execution removes emotion from trading.

My approach centers on Opening Range Breakout strategies combined with VWAP and standard deviation analysis across multiple timeframes. Each indicator is tested against live market conditions on NQ and ES futures before publication.

All indicators are built in PineScript v6 on TradingView with source code available on GitHub. I believe in transparency—traders should understand the tools they rely on.
